About

DjangoCon US will again be held in Portland, Oregon, but this time we are downtown at the Hilton Portland and Executive Tower and close to the famous Pioneer Courthouse Square.

Location

Hilton Portland and Executive Tower
921 SW Sixth Avenue
Portland, OR 97204

Phone: (503) 226-1611
Fax: (503) 220-2565

Rates

Both single and double occupancy rooms will be $104.

A block of rooms have been reserved for September 4th–10th. The special room rate will be available until August 12th or until the group block is sold out, whichever comes first.

Please book your hotel room on the DjangoCon Group Hotel Booking Page. This automatically gives you the conference rate as long as it remains available, and allows us to verify you as a part of the conference room block.

Directions

From the airport via MAX Light Rail: Board MAX Red Line to City Center & Beaverton TC. Get off at Pioneer Square North MAX Station. Walk across Pioneer Courthouse Square southbound. Cost: $2.40.

If you are driving to the hotel: please use their driving directions

Unless otherwise notified your accommodation will be in the hotel main building. You can reserve a room in the Executive Tower, situated kitty-corner from the main building, at a premium rate should you so desire.
